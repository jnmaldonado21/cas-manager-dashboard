What these are

Department staff submitting web update requests to MarComm: new profiles, content edits, accessibility training, broken link reports.

What to watch

High volume from one department = training opportunity. Slow first-response = capacity issue. Ticket spikes may correlate with semester start/end.

The opportunity

Training department editors (Cascade + accessibility) is the highest-leverage investment. Each trained editor reduces recurring tickets and frees MarComm for strategic work.

Capacity model

As department editors are trained and routine work is delegated, ticket volume drops — freeing capacity for strategic quality and enrollment work.
