Context for new managers — what these tickets are
What these are
Department staff (admins, faculty, coordinators) submitting web update requests to the MarComm web team: new profiles, content edits, accessibility training, broken link reports.
What to watch
High ticket volume from the same department may signal a training opportunity. Slow first-response time may signal team capacity issues. Low CSAT (if available) needs investigation.
The opportunity
Training program for department editors (Cascade access + accessibility standards) is the single highest-leverage investment. Each trained editor reduces recurring tickets and frees MarComm for strategic work.
